Replicate Tables 8 and 9 of Wolpin (1982), Yale Economic Growth Center Working Paper No. 424.

This paper was published in the JPE 1984. The published version makes references to these tables but they are not include.

The parameter estimates differ between the working paper and the article. The working paper version are used for the replication, but the published estimates are given as well.

The Model

t = 0, 1, ..., 19, ..., 29.
α = (n), n ∈ {0,1}
A(α) = {0,1} if t< 20
		{0}   if t≥ 20


θ = (M)
ε = (psi;)
Prob(M' = M+1) = pn
M' = M + 1    if t<T
     M        otherwise.
ψ ∼ N(0,1)


 X = Y - ( b(n-d) + (c1It=1 + c2It=2	+
 c30+c31t+c32t2))n


 U = (α1+ψ)M +α2M2   + β1X+β2X2	+
 γ1MX+γ2MS
