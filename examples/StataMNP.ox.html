Replicate the Stata™ example of multinomial probit, using MNP.

Stata example from help mprobit 
 . webuse sysdsn3
 . mprobit insure age male nonwhite site2 site3

The Stata mprobit command assumes independent standard normal error terms:
Stata uses Gauss-Hermite quadrature to compute the log-likelihood.
